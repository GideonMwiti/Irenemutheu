About Me

Hello, I'm Irene Mutheu Sila, a professional model based in Kisii, Kenya. With 2 years of experience in the fashion industry, I've had the privilege of collaborating with renowned designers, photographers, and brands across diverse projects. My modeling journey began with a passion for runway fashion and editorial photography, leading me to work with esteemed clients such as Phanaka Arts Production. My versatility spans various genres, including commercial and runway modeling, allowing me to adapt seamlessly to different creative visions. Beyond the camera, I am committed to promoting body positivity and diversity within the industry. I believe in the power of fashion as a medium for self-expression and cultural representation. In addition to my modeling career, I am pursuing a degree in Project Planning & Management, which has enriched my understanding of the industry from multiple perspectives. Beyond the runway, i am passionate about exploring new cultures through travelling, capturing moments via photography and experimenting with culinary arts I am always eager to connect with fellow creatives and explore new opportunities. Feel free to reach out to discuss potential collaborations or projects.
